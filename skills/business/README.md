# Business Skills

> 한국어: [README.ko.md](./README.ko.md)

Skills for the founder / product / marketing side of building things, for a developer who also
wants to ship and sell products and play CEO/CTO/CFO/PM roles.

**Model-invoked** (used automatically when the context fits)

- [biz-opportunity-scout](./biz-opportunity-scout/SKILL.md) — Validate a business opportunity with TAM/SAM/SOM, unit economics, competitive read, and PMF signals; honest Go/No-Go.
- [marketing-copy](./marketing-copy/SKILL.md) — Turn a product/feature into Korean marketing copy (landing, launch post, SNS, value prop).
- [product-spec-builder](./product-spec-builder/SKILL.md) — Turn a rough idea into a buildable PRD via a short interview, sliced into shippable units.

All are currently **Review** level (under evaluation) until tested in real use.
